Creation

This is the first game I've made outside of school, and it was for a three-day game jam hosted by B3agz (youtube channel) and was published on February 28th, 2021. The theme for this game jam was "Swarms." In total, there were three people who worked on this game, and three playtesters.

  • Alec (me): The lead programmer
  • Erik: The secondary programmer and main musician.
  • Jaden: The tertiary programmer and main artist.
  • Joseph: A playtester.
  • Adam: A playtester.
  • Jake: A playtester.

Game Description

The Hive is a bee-themed tower defense where you play as a worker bee attempting to protect the hive from various aggressive enemies. You can build various different towers, such as the Honeycomb Wall, Bee Defender, Sticky Trap, Mender Bee, and Golden Mine. There are two different modes; Campaign and Endless. C ampaign lasts for 20 preset waves of enemies, while the endless mode algorithmically generates waves forever.
