Death, Paperwork, and Minigames

Game Link

Creation

This is another two-day game jam that is hosted by BattleblockBOss and was published on July 3rd, 2021. The theme for this game jam was "Death is a Luxury Few can Afford". In total, there were 3 people who worked on this game.

  • Alec (me): The lead programmer.
  • Jaden: The lead artist and designer.
  • Erik: The lead musician.

Game Description

Death, Paperwork, and Minigames is a game where you play as the Grimm Reaper, filling out the paperwork of dead people. You can increase their net worth by playing minigames. There are three minigames that are currently playable. There's a quickdraw minigame, cake stacking minigame, and a driving minigame.
