Death, Paperwork, and Minigames

The fourth game I've made was for a two-day game jam and was published on July 3rd, 2021. You can play Death, Paperwork, and Minigames here.

Death, Paperwork, and Minigames is a game where you play as the Grimm Reaper, filling out the paperwork of dead people. You can increase their net worth by playing minigames.
