Creation

This is the second game I've made outside of school, and it was for a week-long game jam that was hosted by SkyanSam (youtube channel) and was published on April 22nd, 2021. The theme for this game jam was "10 seconds". In total, there were only 2 people who worked on this game.

  • Alec (me): The lead programmer and 3D modeler.
  • Erik: The secondary programmer and musician.

Game Description

Deletion is a bullet-hell-themed platformer about a piece of code trying to escape a computer. You play in third person with the ability to slow down time, regenerate health, and gain a speed boost. However, you only get 10 seconds of total time to do any of these actions per level. Each level can have different objectives; Survive, Traverse, or Both. In survival levels, enemies will spawn with the intent to kill you, and you must survive for a set period of time. In traverse levels, you must go from the spawn position to the end goal. In the final level, you must traverse various platforms while dodging enemies.
