Creation

This is the third game I've made outside of school, and it was for a 48-hour game jam that was hosted by GTMK (youtube channel) and was published on June 13th, 2021. The theme for this game was "Joined Together". In total, there were 2 people who worked on this game, and 1 playtester.

  • Alec (me): The lead programmer.
  • Erik: The secondary programmer, main visual designer, and musician.
  • Joseph: The playtester.
