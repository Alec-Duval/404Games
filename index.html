Welcome All!

My name is Alec Duval, and I am a game programmer and designer. I started programming around Summer of 2018 (just before high school). Since then, I have made many "games". This site is for keeping track of all my (mostly) finished games. For the record, every game here was made during a game jam, so the games may not be bug-free nor completely polished.

Games


The Hive: A bee-themed tower defense.

Deletion: A bullet hell where you must try to escape a computers antivirus.

Snowstorm: A simple platformer where you must shift space and time to traverse a blizzard and return home.

Death, Paperwork, and Minigames: Play as the Grimm Reaper doing paperwork to send the rich to heaven and the poor to hell.

Entombed: A desert-themed rougelike to test the morality of the player.
